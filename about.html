ABOUT COTI HOLDING

COTI Origin

COTI Holding is a UAE-based investment and operating company focused on the alternative nicotine category. The company operates through a fully integrated ecosystem that combines advanced manufacturing, distribution, and retail capabilities. Through its core verticals — Next Gen, ANDS, and SC Labs — COTI provides partners with a unified platform for market entry, scale, and compliance across the MENA and broader regional markets. The company is committed to innovation, regulatory alignment, and responsible industry practices.

THE ARCHITECTURAL AUTHORITY

Strategic Core

COTI operates through a set of specialized verticals, each fulfilling a defined and complementary role within the broader platform — collectively enabling brands to scale efficiently and responsibly.

Vision

To become the leading regional platform and a global reference point for integrated solutions in alternative nicotine — bringing industry, science, and regulation to define the future of the category.

Mission

To unify manufacturing, distribution, and retail into a single integrated platform that enables alternative nicotine brands to scale efficiently, operate responsibly, and deliver consistent, high-quality experiences across markets.

Purpose

To structure and enable the responsible growth of alternative nicotine delivery solutions through a unified, compliant, and scalable ecosystem.

IDENTITY

Role In The Industry

COTI Holding was established in response to a clear structural gap within the regional alternative nicotine landscape:

the absence of a single, trusted platform capable of integrating manufacturing excellence, distribution scale, and retail discipline within a unified framework.

COTI exists to enable the next generation of nicotine brands to scale responsibly and sustainably — contributing to the transition beyond combustion while advancing higher standards, stronger alignment, and long-term credibility across the category.

Headquartered in Dubai, COTI operates as a holding company bringing together the full value chain under centralized, compliance-first structure.

ABOUT COTI HOLDING

COTI Origin

COTI Holding is a UAE-based investment and operating company focused on the alternative nicotine category. Founded in response to a clear structural gap within the regional landscape, the company brings together a fully integrated ecosystem combining advanced manufacturing, distribution, and retail capabilities.

Through its core verticals — Next Gen for manufacturing, ANDS for distribution, and SC Labs for retail intelligence — COTI provides partners with a unified platform for market entry, scale, and compliance across the MENA region and broader regional markets.

The company is committed to innovation, regulatory alignment, and responsible industry practices, positioning itself as the structural authority for alternative nicotine across the region.

VISION

Setting the regional reference for alternative nicotine.

To become the leading regional platform and a global reference point for integrated solutions in alternative nicotine — bringing industry, science, and regulation together to define the future of the category.

Our vision is anchored in three commitments: integration (one platform across the value chain), credibility (compliance-first execution), and responsibility (advancing the transition beyond combustion in a regulated, accountable way).

By 2030, we intend to be the partner of choice for any brand, operator, or authority working on alternative nicotine in MENA and adjacent travel-retail markets.

MISSION

Unify manufacturing, distribution, and retail under one operating system.

To unify manufacturing, distribution, and retail into a single integrated platform that enables alternative nicotine brands to scale efficiently, operate responsibly, and deliver consistent, high-quality experiences across markets.

We do this by owning and orchestrating each layer of the value chain — production at Next Gen, market access through ANDS, and consumer interface via SC Labs — tied together by a shared compliance and governance backbone.

The mission is operational: less time to market, lower compliance friction, and more reliable insight loops between consumers and product development.

PURPOSE

Enable responsible growth of alternative nicotine.

To structure and enable the responsible growth of alternative nicotine delivery solutions through a unified, compliant, and scalable ecosystem.

COTI exists to bridge the gap between product innovation and regulatory integrity — supporting the transition beyond combustion while protecting consumer safety, market discipline, and youth-protection standards.

Every initiative we take — from manufacturing standards to retail audits — is judged against this purpose first.
