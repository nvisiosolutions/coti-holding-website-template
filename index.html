Who We Are

COTI Holding is a UAE-based investment and operating company focused on the alternative nicotine category.

The company operates through a fully integrated ecosystem that combines advanced manufacturing, distribution, and retail capabilities. Through its core verticals — Next Gen, ANDS, and SC Labs; COTI provides partners with a unified platform for market entry, scale, and compliance across the MENA and broader regional markets. The company is committed to innovation, regulatory alignment, and responsible industry practices.

ECOSYSTEM — MANUFACTURING

NextGen — Manufacturing

NextGen is COTI's manufacturing vertical — GMP-aligned facilities producing alternative nicotine products for regional brands and contract partners. Capabilities include device assembly, e-liquid formulation and filling, pouch manufacturing, and packaging — all under one quality system with end-to-end traceability.

Production schedules are tied to ANDS demand signals, so volume scales with real distribution velocity rather than forecast guesswork.

ECOSYSTEM — RETAIL

SC Labs — Retail Intelligence

SC Labs operates COTI's owned retail environments and partner concept stores, designed as both points of sale and live observation labs for the category. Each store captures structured consumer signal — preferences, conversion, objections, demographic patterns — that feeds the central insights pipeline.

That intelligence loops back into R&D priorities and Next Gen production mix, closing the recursive feedback cycle.

ECOSYSTEM — DISTRIBUTION

ANDS — Distribution

ANDS is COTI's distribution arm — a multi-category logistics and market-access platform connecting manufacturing output to regulated retail channels across MENA. ANDS handles import licensing, bonded warehousing, last-mile fulfilment, and channel compliance for traditional retail, modern trade, and travel-retail partners.

Every shipment is traceable end-to-end, with batch-level recall capability and full regulatory documentation on demand.
