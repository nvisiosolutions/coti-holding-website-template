FOUNDATION

R&D — The structural foundation

R&D anchors the COTI ecosystem. Every product entering the platform passes through scientific validation, regulatory mapping, and feasibility review before it ever reaches manufacturing.

Our research programs cover formulation science, device engineering, harm-reduction studies, and category-level regulatory monitoring across MENA and adjacent markets.

R&D outputs feed directly into NextGen production specifications, and insights from SC Labs retail loop back into the research priorities.

MANUFACTURING

Next Gen — Production at scale

Next Gen is COTI's manufacturing vertical — GMP-aligned facilities producing alternative nicotine products for regional brands and contract partners.

Capabilities include device assembly, e-liquid formulation and filling, pouch manufacturing, and packaging — all under one quality system with end-to-end traceability.

Production schedules are tied to ANDS demand signals, so volume scales with real distribution velocity rather than forecast guesswork.

DISTRIBUTION

ANDS — Regional distribution

ANDS is COTI's distribution arm — a multi-category logistics and market-access platform connecting manufacturing output to regulated retail channels across MENA.

ANDS handles import licensing, bonded warehousing, last-mile fulfilment, and channel compliance for traditional retail, modern trade, and travel-retail partners.

Every shipment is traceable end-to-end, with batch-level recall capability and full regulatory documentation on demand.

RETAIL

SC Labs — The retail intelligence layer

SC Labs operates COTI's owned retail environments and partner concept stores, designed as both points of sale and live observation labs for the category.

Each store captures structured consumer signal — preferences, conversion, objections, demographic patterns — that feeds the central insights pipeline.

That intelligence loops back into R&D priorities and Next Gen production mix, closing the recursive feedback cycle.

FEEDBACK

Consumer Intelligence — The recursive loop

Consumer Intelligence is the data layer connecting every other vertical. Real-time signal from SC Labs retail, distribution velocity from ANDS, and field studies from R&D all converge here.

We use this to drive product decisions, regulatory positioning, and partner roadmaps — replacing slow, anecdotal feedback with structured, real-time insight.

The result: a category that learns and adapts continuously, with the governance and compliance discipline regulators expect.
