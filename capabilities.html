Strategic Mandate

COTI Holding is mandated to build, integrate, and scale a controlled value chain across alternative nicotine, owning and aligning manufacturing, distribution, and retail to ensure product integrity, market discipline, and consistent execution across all markets.

This mandate is executed through three interdependent verticals:

Manufacturing, Production & Product Control

COTI develops, produces, and oversees alternative nicotine products, combining in-house manufacturing, production capabilities, and R&D to ensure quality, consistency, and alignment with evolving regulatory standards at source. This includes product development, validation, and continuous improvement across the portfolio.

Distribution & Market Access

COTI structures and operates distribution networks that provide controlled market access, ensuring products are positioned, supplied, and scaled in line with market dynamics and strategic objectives.

Retail & Consumer Interface

COTI owns and manages retail environments that define the end-consumer experience, ensuring responsible sales practices, brand consistency, and direct feedback loops into the platform.
